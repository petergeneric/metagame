NY Noir: People Properties

Ethnicities

The following lists the ethnicities in the NY Noir People Database. It is used to generate names, so might be useful for authors looking for a particular name background
  • irish

Gender

NY Noir holds three genders: male, female, and ungendered. Ungendered entries appear with names that could be either gender, so authors are free to interpret them as they wish. These gender tags are used to generate forenames.
